Attack on Titan is a Japanese manga series created by Hajime Isayama. The story is set in a world where humanity resides within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from the Titans, gigantic humanoid creatures that devour humans seemingly without reason. The narrative follows Eren Yeager and his friends who join the military to fight the Titans after their hometown is destroyed. As the story unfolds, it explores themes of survival, freedom, and the mysteries behind the origin of the Titans.
